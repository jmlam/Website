Howdy! I'm Jeremiah, an amateur artist, runner, and gamer. I have a love for shounen managa and itss muscular figures which has led me down the dark path I tread to this day. I'm a huge fan of Murata-sensei, the artist of Onepunch-Man and I greatly admire his detailed shading work, extremely expressive figures, and overall artistic ability. I hope someday that I can become an artist of that caliber and creativity. Currently, my favorite mediums are pencil and pen, and I detest stipiling. Nice to meet you, please enjoy the site.
